Brief of Aminu waziri Tambuwal

Aminu Waziri Tambuwal, Governor Of Sokoto State And Former Speaker Of The House Of Representatives, Was Born On 10th  Jan 1966. By 2004, He Became The Minority Leader Of The House. Tambuwal Is A Man Of Many Parts Who Has Been Able To Distinguish Himself In All Of Them.

Born on 10th Jan 1966, Aminu Waziri Tambuwal’s parents had a premonition that their son was destined to be a leader and counsellor to great men, so they named him Aminu meaning the trustworthy and dependable. That premonition has been the lot of RT Hon Aminu Waziri Tambuwal, CFR, incumbent Governor of Sokoto State and former Speaker of the House of Representatives.

Long before his birth, his family had maintained a strong voice in the palace of leaders, main reason they hold the title of “ Waziri” meaning Prime Minister or counsellors of great leaders. Trained as a lawyer at Usman Danfodiyo University Sokoto, this urbane and avid believer in the integrity of hard work and merit in public service, has lived a life of selflessness and sacrifice for the good of the greater society……

impact

 

Governor Tambuwal with excellent political dexterity, deepened our democratic practice for more transparent and productive governance by reinforcing the principle of separation of powers as well as asserting the independence of the legislature as enshrined in the 1999 Constitution (as amended).

Governor

Rt. Hon Aminu Waziri Tambuwal, CFR

On April 11, 2015, he was elected Governor, Sokoto State under the banner of the All Progressives Congress (APC). Reelected In 2019 under the umbrella of the People’s Democratic Party (PDP), currently the Chairman of the PDP Governors Forum and Deputy Chairman of Nigeria Governors Forum (NGF).

As Governor, Rt. Hon. Aminu Waziri Tambuwal continues to display a remarkable commitment towards the wellbeing of his people as evident in his developmental projects in critical sectors including education, healthcare, agriculture, infrastructure and urban and rural development among others…

Speaker

Rt. Hon Aminu Waziri Tambuwal, CFR

A strong and independent Legislature elected to represent Kebbe/Tambuwal Federal constituency of Sokoto State in 2003 on the platform of the All Nigeria Peoples Party, ANPP.
By 2004, he became the Minority Leader of the House, recontest for the Kebbe/Tambuwal Federal Constituency under the umbrella of the People’s Democratic Party, PDP and easily elected as Deputy Chief Whip of the House, which was the only Principal Office position zoned to the North West at that time, In 2011, Rt. Hon. Aminu Waziri Tambuwal became one of the most experienced and ranking among members of the House.
It is the reason when the time came he was elected as a Speaker of the House, the preferred candidate by his colleagues, their trust lead him to the 7th House of Representatives as Speaker.

The Ideal Candidate For President

My experience and expertise in administration as a lawyer, former Speaker of the National Assembly current Governor of Sokoto State make me a strong contestant among the candidates for the position of Presidency.

I graduated with an LLB (Hons) degree in 1991 and was called to the Nigerian Bar after completing my legal studies at the Nigerian Law School, Lagos, a year later where I obtained Barrister at Law, BL. I am an active member of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA), at the State and national levels. First Public Relations Officer between 1996 and 1997. Sokoto State Branch Secretary (1997–1998), Member of the Constitution Review Committee of the NBA (1997–1998).  Assistant National Financial Secretary (1998–2000) and Assistant National Secretary NBA. A Member of Life Benchers Body of, Nigeria; Member International Bar Association, and Member American Bar Association. So I can contribute a lot to the improvement of our society at large for the better Nigeria. Thank you.
